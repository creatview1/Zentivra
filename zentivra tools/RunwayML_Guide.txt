3. KEY FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✦ Gen-3 Alpha (Text to Video)
  Describe చేస్తే - cinematic video create అవుతుంది.
  "A woman walking through a field of sunflowers, golden hour, 
   slow motion, film grain" - actual video వస్తుంది!

✦ Image to Video
  మీ photo upload చేస్తే - అది animate అవుతుంది.
  Still landscape photo → moving video with wind, water movement

✦ Video to Video (Style Transfer)
  Existing video ని different artistic style లో render చేయవచ్చు.
  "నా video ని anime style లో convert చేయి"
  "Oil painting look ఇవ్వు"

✦ Green Screen (Background Removal)
  Automatically background remove చేస్తుంది.
  After Effects అక్కర్లేదు - one click!

✦ Inpainting
  Video లో specific object remove చేయవచ్చు.
  "ఆ car ని video నుండి erase చేయి" - done!

✦ Expand (Outpainting for Video)
  Video frame extend చేయవచ్చు.
  16:9 to 9:16 (vertical) convert!

✦ Motion Brush
  Image లో specific areas ని different directions లో animate చేయవచ్చు.
  "Clouds ని left to right move చేయి, trees still ఉండనీయి"

✦ Infinite Zoom
  Never-ending zoom effect - psychedelic visuals
  Music videos కోసం great!

✦ Audio to Video (Coming Features)
  Music based visual generation
  Automatic sync with beats

5. STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: Account Create
  → runwayml.com కి వెళ్ళండి
  → "Get Started Free" click చేయండి
  → Google account తో login చేయండి
  → Free account: Limited credits

STEP 2: Gen-3 Alpha Explore
  → Dashboard లో "Gen-3 Alpha" select చేయండి
  → Text prompt option choose చేయండి

STEP 3: Prompt Write చేయండి
  → Camera movement describe చేయండి: "slow zoom in", "pan left"
  → Subject: "a woman", "a mountain", "a city at night"
  → Style: "cinematic", "documentary", "surreal"
  → Lighting: "golden hour", "neon lights", "foggy morning"
  
  Example: "A slow zoom into an ancient Telugu temple, 
            misty morning, golden sunlight through trees, 
            cinematic 35mm film grain"

STEP 4: Duration & Settings
  → 4 seconds లేదా 16 seconds select చేయండి
  → Resolution: 720p (standard) లేదా 4K (pro)

STEP 5: Generate
  → "Generate" click చేయండి
  → 2-5 minutes wait చేయండి (quality takes time!)
  → Video preview చేయండి

STEP 6: Refine
  → Same video ని different variation generate చేయండి
  → Prompt adjust చేయండి
  → Several attempts లో best one select చేయండి

STEP 7: Download
  → Download button click చేయండి
  → MP4 format లో saves అవుతుంది

💡 Camera Motion Keywords వాడండి:
  "slow pan right", "drone shot ascending", "dolly zoom", 
  "handheld camera", "static shot", "tracking shot"

💡 "Cinematic" Keyword Always Add చేయండి
  Quality dramatically improve అవుతుంది.

💡 Image to Video for Better Results
  Text to video కంటే, image to video more consistent results.
  First Midjourney లో image create చేయండి, తర్వాత Runway లో animate చేయండి!

💡 Short, Clear Prompts
  Too long prompts sometimes confuse AI.
  Key elements focus చేయండి.

💡 Multiple Generations
  Same prompt తో 3-4 times generate చేయండి - best one వస్తుంది.

💡 Combine with Video Editors
  Runway clips + CapCut/Premiere Pro = Professional videos!
