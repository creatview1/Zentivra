Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience across visits.

2. How Zentivra Uses Cookies

We use only essential, first-party browser storage (localStorage) to save your theme preference (dark/light mode). We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

3. Essential Storage

Theme preference — Stored locally to remember your chosen display mode between visits. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party websites linked from Zentivra (such as AI tool providers) may use their own cookies. We have no control over these cookies. Please refer to each tool's cookie policy for details.

5. Managing Cookies

You can clear localStorage and cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing your theme preference will reset the Platform to its default dark mode on your next visit.
