Section 04

Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember certain information about your visit, making the experience smoother and more personalized. Zentivra uses cookies for several purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functionality of the website. They cannot be turned off as they enable core features like page navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies, often set by services like Google Analytics, help us understand how visitors interact with our site — for example, which pages are visited most and how long people stay. All data collected is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by our advertising partners (such as Google AdSense) to deliver ads that are relevant to your interests. These cookies may track your browsing behavior across other websites as well.
  • Preference Cookies: These remember your settings and preferences (such as region or language) to improve your experience on future visits.
Your Cookie Choices: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies at any time. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website or the quality of your experience.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Common instructions for popular browsers:

Google Chrome Mozilla Firefox Apple Safari Microsoft Edge Opera

For more information about how to manage cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org or your browser's official help documentation.

We also support opt-out tools provided by industry organizations such as the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) and the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA), which allow you to opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads: You can visit adssettings.google.com to adjust your Google ad personalization settings. You can also visit optout.networkadvertising.org to opt out of interest-based advertising from NAI member companies.

Please note that even if you opt out of personalized advertising, you may still see generic, non-targeted ads while browsing Zentivra.
